Personnel[edit]

According to Mark Lewisohn[1] and Walter Everett:[2]

  • Paul McCartney – lead vocal, backing vocal, lead/rhythm guitar
  • John Lennon – backing vocal, six-string bass, sound effects (through tenor saxophone mouthpiece), piano[3]
  • George Harrison – backing vocal, lead/rhythm guitar, slide guitar
  • Ringo Starr – drums, vocal shout
  • Mal Evans – trumpet
  1. ^ Lewisohn 2005, p. 154.
  2. ^ Everett 1999, pp. 191, 347.
  3. ^ Howlett, Kevin (2018). The Beatles (50th Anniversary Super Deluxe Version) (book). Apple Records.
