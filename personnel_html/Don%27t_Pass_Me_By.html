Personnel[edit]

  • Ringo Starr – double-tracked vocals, drums, tack piano, sleigh bells, cowbell, maracas, congas
  • Paul McCartney – grand piano, bass guitar
  • Jack Fallon – violin

The pianos were both recorded into a Leslie 147 speaker.
