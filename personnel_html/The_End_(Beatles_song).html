Personnel[edit]

Personnel above per Ian MacDonald[1] and Kenneth Womack[2]

  • Paul McCartney – lead and backing vocals, bass guitar, piano, guitar solo (Epiphone Casino)[3]
  • John Lennon – harmony vocals, rhythm guitar, guitar solo (Epiphone Casino)[4]
  • George Harrison – harmony vocals, rhythm and lead guitar, guitar solo (Gibson Les Paul)[5]
  • Ringo Starr – drums, tambourine[6]
  • George Martin – orchestration[7]
  • uncredited – 12 violins, four violas, one double-bass, four horns, three trumpets, one trombone, one bass trombone[6]
