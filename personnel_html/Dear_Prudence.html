Personnel[edit]

The Beatles

  • John Lennon – double-tracked lead vocal, backing vocal,[2] rhythm guitars
  • Paul McCartney – harmony and backing vocals,[3] drums, bass guitar, piano, tambourine,[4] handclaps
  • George Harrison – harmony and backing vocals, rhythm guitar,[3] lead guitars, tambourine,[4] handclaps

Additional contributors

  • Mal Evans, Jackie Lomax, John McCartney – backing vocals, handclaps,[2] unspecified percussion[5]
