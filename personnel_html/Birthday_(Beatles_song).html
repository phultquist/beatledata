Personnel[edit]

According to Ian MacDonald:[1]

The Beatles

  • Paul McCartney – lead vocal, lead guitar, piano, handclaps
  • John Lennon – lead and backing vocals, lead guitar, handclaps
  • George Harrison – six-string bass, handclaps
  • Ringo Starr – drums, tambourine, handclaps

Additional participants

  • Pattie Harrison – backing vocal, handclaps
  • Yoko Ono – backing vocal, handclaps
  • Mal Evans – handclaps

However, according to the book accompanying the 2018 box set The Beatles: Super Deluxe Version, the annotation on the tape box from the session offers an alternative line-up that "explodes some myths of who played what":[2]

  • Paul McCartney – lead vocal, bass, piano
  • John Lennon – vocals, guitar
  • George Harrison – guitar, tambourine
  • Ringo Starr – drums, handclaps
  • Pattie Harrison – backing vocal
  • Yoko Ono – backing vocal
  • Mal Evans – handclaps
