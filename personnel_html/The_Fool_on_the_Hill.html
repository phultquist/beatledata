Personnel[edit]

According to Ian MacDonald (except where noted), the following musicians played on the Beatles' recording:[1]

The Beatles

  • Paul McCartney – vocals, piano, acoustic guitar, recorder, bass, penny whistle[2]
  • John Lennon – harmonica, Jew's harp
  • George Harrison – 12-string acoustic guitar,[3] harmonica
  • Ringo Starr – drums, maracas, finger cymbals

Additional musicians

  • Christopher Taylor, Richard Taylor, Jack Ellory – flutes
  1. ^ MacDonald 1998, p. 237.
  2. ^ George Martin interviewed on the Pop Chronicles (1969)
  3. ^ Everett 1999, p. 138.
