Personnel[edit]

  • Paul McCartney – lead and background vocals, bass guitar, acoustic guitar, handclaps, sound effects
  • John Lennon – double-tracked lead vocals (middle eight), backing vocals, ukulele, banjo, harmonica, handclaps, sound effects
  • George Harrison – backing vocals, acoustic guitar, handclaps, sound effects
  • Ringo Starr – backing vocals, drums, percussion, handclaps, sound effects

Mark Lewisohn reported that the "party-style" backing vocals were provided by "whoever happened to be around".[1]
