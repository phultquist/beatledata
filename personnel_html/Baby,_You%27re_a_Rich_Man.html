Personnel[edit]

According to Mark Lewisohn[1] and Ian MacDonald:[2]

The Beatles

  • John Lennon – double-tracked lead vocal, piano, clavioline
  • Paul McCartney – backing vocals, bass, piano
  • George Harrison – backing vocals, rhythm guitar,[3] lead guitar,[4] handclaps
  • Ringo Starr – drums, tambourine, maracas, handclaps

Additional musicians

  • Eddie Kramer – vibraphone
  • Mick Jagger – backing vocals[nb 1]
